Experimental Webpage

by David J. Hammaker

I made this webpage as a practice webpage. Here, I can experiment with different methods, styles, and hopefully some Python and JavaScript, just for the practice. When I was trying to think of a topic for the webpage, I struggled for a while, but I finally settled on showcasing my artwork. In the future, I may also add bits of my written works, also.

So far, I'm very proud of the columns shown on this page. On a computer screen, you can see three distinct columns. The one on the left has a few of my paintings. In the center, obviously, I have text content. Then on the right, I have an "About Me" section. It was tricky, but I finally managed to style the three columns in such a way that they will collapse gracefully on narrower screens. On such a screen, there may be two, or even only one, column. But the sections are still there, with that beautiful header at the top, with the faded background that took some research and experimentation to figure out (like most things on this page).
